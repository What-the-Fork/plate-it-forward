Plate it Forward: Consumer Persona

Jodi McKnight

Technology:

  • No (charged) personal devices (has a basic pay-as-you-go mobile from Walmart a few years ago; could no longer afford to keep paying for service and also eventually lost the charger; kept the phone in hopes of finding another charger for it one day)
  • Beginner user with limited access to computers/phones at community centers, shelters, and libraries

Attitudes and Behaviors:

  • Unhoused for a couple of years now and bounces between camps/shelters
  • Difficulty focusing and maintaining energy levels to do anything because she's constantly starving and feeling ill from infrequent and poor-quality meals
  • Enjoys talking to strangers and learning about their stories, and has even made a few acquaintances along the way
  • No family in town, and is too proud/embarrassed to reach out to family in another state (likes to Google them though when she gets access to a computer)

Frustrations:

  • Infrequent meals
  • People and restaurants wasting so much food
  • Difficulty finding/traveling to places that offer free services/resources
  • Bouncing between shelters/not having a steady living situation

Goals:

  • Eat at least one decent meal per day so she can focus on improving her situation
  • Easily find/travel to free services/resources
  • Find a steady living situation
  • Get a job

User Story

"As a user, I want to easily find a place near me that offers free food."
