Persona

Name: Cori Taylor

Cori is the Community Outreach Director at Community Center B.

Devices:

Cori has a desktop computer in her office, uses a Chromebook provided by the community center to work from home when needed, and has an iPhone 12 as a personal phone.

Proficiency:

Cori uses Microsoft 365 apps like Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook to on her work desktop and Chromebook for various work tasks and office needs. She uses her phone to browse the web and check her social media accounts. Cori also uses Facebook and Instagram to share events happening soon at the community center, and stay updated on other events going on around the city.

Frustrations and Needs:

Cori believes community-led events and businesses are the future. She likes to absorb as much information about what is happening around the city as possible. She is frustrated by the little support or understanding of community within her organization, so she researches new opportunities for outreach on her own.

Goals:

Cori wants to find restaurants that can donate food to provide for a free lunch program at the community center. She wants to connect with restaurants in the same neighborhood, so it will be easy for donation deliver or pickup.

User-story:

"As a user, I want to find restaurants, in the same neighborhood as the community center, that would be willing to donate food for the adult/family free lunch program at the center."

Precondition:

Cori is on the Plate It Forward homepage.

Post condtion:

Cori has accepted a partner proposal from a new restaurant and updated her meal chart from her latest free lunch event at the center.

Interaction Flow:

  • Cori scans the homepage and clicks the "Partner Portal" link in the navbar.
  • The site brings up a login modal.
  • She types in her email and password and clicks the button to login.
  • The site directs her to her community center profile page.
  • Cori scans the profile page and sees that a new restaurant wants to partner with the center! She accepts the proposal by clicking the accept button.
  • The site clears the notification and notifies Cori that she has accepted the proposal.
  • Cori goes to the meal chart and updates the number of meals provided and that date meals were provided for her current partnering restaurants.
  • She logs out of her profile by clicking the "logout" link in the navbar.
  • The site redirects Cori back to the site homepage.
