Plate-It-Forward

David Rodriguez

Profession

  • Owns Taquito Amigos, a local mexican restaurant
  • Business has stayed steady through the pandemic, and is looking to contribute to his community.

Technology

  • PC power user
  • Comfortable with Point of Sale devices in restaurant.
  • Manages necessary paperwork, including sales reports, inventory, and staff attendance reports through computer software.
  • Monitors restaurant finances, including sales and finances.

Attitudes and Behaviors

  • Dedicated father and husband who does his best to empathize with the underprivileged.
  • Feels like he can do more to address food insecurity in community.
  • Works with outreach programs that aim to provide a safe space after school for low income students to learn cooking skills.
  • Provides work opportunities for non-violent felons.
  • Donates to local shelters that provide temporary housing and support for individuals who are homeless.

Frustrations and Needs

  • Frustrated with the lack of funding for social programs in Albuquerque.
  • Is not content with level of transparency from local social programs.
  • Feels that food assistance programs aren't as accessible to those in need.
  • Wants an easier way to collaborate with organizations that provide food for those in need.
  • Needs a way to communicate with community centers when managing his restaurant during busy season.

Goals

  • Streamline communications with community centers in the city.
  • Inspire other businesses to contribute to food assistance programs.
  • Provide direct access to food for individuals in need.
  • Track success and learn from failures in his aim to support community food assistance.

User Story

"As a user, I want an easier way to communicate with community centers that provide food for those in need."

Use Case and Pre

Pre-Condition

David is on Plate It Forward main page

Post-Condition

David has successfully partnered with local Community Center

Interaction Flow

  • David reads over main page and clicks partner with us on nav bar
  • Site redirects to Partner with us page
  • David reads information and clicks become a partner today button
  • Site redirects to modal "Become a partner today"
  • David inputs restaurant information, eg. name, email, phone number,
  • Site redirects to congratulations page for partner submission
  • David receives notification of acceptance and clicks account creation button
  • Site redirects to account creation page
  • David creates user id/password and clicks create account button
  • Site redirects to partner profile page
  • David sees they are now partnered with local community center
